    Raaja by RaGa

    Raaja by RaGa -- A Carnatic celebration of Ilayaraaja. Presented by Eppo, this first of it's kind US concert tour of Ranjani-Gayatri is a celebration of Isaigyani Ilayaraaja. Each concert will feature his various compositions in films and outside of it in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada and Malayalam, carefully curated, explained and presented with the classical interpretations by the versatile virtuoso duo "Ranjani Gayatri" and their team of co-artists. It showcases a hereto unexplored point-of-view to Ilayaraaja's hugely popular music thus making it much more interesting and intellectually stimulating for the listeners.
